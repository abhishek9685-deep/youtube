Competition Guidelines

  • Mobile Phones: Phones must be turned off and submitted to staff upon arrival. No electronic devices are allowed during the competition.
  • Arrival Time: Teams must arrive before the reporting time; latecomers will be disqualified.
  • Start Time: Competition starts at 11:00 AM sharp. Be on time to avoid disqualification.
  • Scoring: Each question is worth 1 point. No negative marking for the first three wrong answers. From the fourth incorrect answer onward, 1 point will be deducted for each wrong response.
  • Mode: The competition is offline, and all participants must attend in person.
  • Identification: Participants must wear their uniforms and carry ID cards.
  • Tie-Breaker: An extra question will be used to break ties.
  • Lunch: No scheduled lunch break; manage your time accordingly.
  • Conduct: Cheating or misconduct will lead to disqualification. Maintain sportsmanship.
  • Jury Decision: The juryâ€™s decisions are final and binding.
  • Equipment: No personal devices are allowed; organizers will provide necessary materials.

Quiz Competition Methodology

  • Team Composition: Teams consist of 2-3 members.
  • Team Registration: Teams will receive a number based on their registration.
  • Competition Structure:
    • First Round: Three parts, each consisting of 20 questions. The top 2 teams from each part will advance to the final round.
    • Final Round: The final round will determine the winner, first runner-up, and second runner-up.
  • Rounds Schedule:
    • Part 1: 11:00 AM - 11:45 AM
    • Part 2: 12:00 PM - 12:45 PM
    • Part 3: 1:00 PM - 1:45 PM
    • Final Round: 2:15 PM - 3:00 PM
  • Question Types: A mix of multiple-choice and true/false questions. Some rounds may include audio-visual questions.
  • Answering Questions: Teams must press a buzzer to answer. The first team to buzz will get 30 seconds to respond.
  • Scoring: Correct answers are worth 1 point. No negative marking for the first two wrong answers, but a deduction of 1 point for subsequent incorrect answers.
  • Tie-Breaker: In the event of a tie, a special question will be given, and the jury will decide the winner based on the correct answer.
